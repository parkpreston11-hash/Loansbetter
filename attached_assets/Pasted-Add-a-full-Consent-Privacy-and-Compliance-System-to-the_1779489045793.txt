Add a full Consent, Privacy, and Compliance System to the lead CRM and marketplace platform.

IMPORTANT:
This platform handles mortgage and financial inquiry leads. The system must be designed to collect clear user consent before any lead sharing, transfer, or partner distribution occurs.

The platform should feel legitimate, transparent, and compliant.

--------------------------------------------------
1. LEAD FORM CONSENT SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------

On every lead submission form, add:

- clear consent disclosure text
- required checkbox
- links to:
  - Privacy Policy
  - Terms of Service

Checkbox must NOT be pre-checked.

Users must manually agree before submission.

--------------------------------------------------
2. CONSENT DISCLOSURE TEXT
--------------------------------------------------

Display the following type of disclosure near submit button:

“By submitting this form, you agree that LoansBetter and its approved lending or marketing partners may contact you by phone, text message, or email regarding mortgage-related products and services using the information provided. Consent is not a condition of purchase.”

Style this professionally and clearly.

--------------------------------------------------
3. CONSENT RECORD STORAGE
--------------------------------------------------

For every submitted lead, automatically store:

- timestamp
- IP address
- consent status
- exact disclosure version accepted
- source page/form

Store this in secure audit logs.

--------------------------------------------------
4. PRIVACY POLICY PAGE
--------------------------------------------------

Create a professional Privacy Policy page explaining:
- what information is collected
- how information is used
- how leads may be shared with approved partners
- opt-out process
- data retention practices

Use clean professional formatting.

--------------------------------------------------
5. TERMS OF SERVICE PAGE
--------------------------------------------------

Create a Terms of Service page covering:
- platform usage
- user responsibilities
- limitations of liability
- lead handling policies
- compliance expectations

--------------------------------------------------
6. LEAD SHARING / TRANSFER RULES
--------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT SYSTEM RULE:

Only leads with verified consent for partner sharing may be:
- transferred
- redistributed
- assigned to external lending partners

If consent does NOT allow partner sharing:
- disable transfer option automatically

--------------------------------------------------
7. MARKETPLACE COMPLIANCE CHECK
--------------------------------------------------

Before any lead transfer:
- verify consent status
- verify approved buyer account
- log transfer history
- save timestamp and company receiving lead

--------------------------------------------------
8. USER OPT-OUT SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------

Add ability for users to:
- request removal
- opt out of communications
- revoke consent

System should flag opted-out leads automatically.

--------------------------------------------------
9. ADMIN COMPLIANCE DASHBOARD
--------------------------------------------------

Admins should be able to:
- view consent logs
- view transfer history
- monitor opt-outs
- audit lead activity
- verify compliance status

--------------------------------------------------
10. UI / DESIGN
--------------------------------------------------

Design should feel:
- professional fintech platform
- trustworthy
- transparent
- legally organized
- modern SaaS style

--------------------------------------------------
FINAL GOAL
--------------------------------------------------

Create a legitimate, consent-based mortgage lead platform with:
- clear opt-in disclosures
- audit logging
- compliant lead transfer workflows
- privacy and terms pages
- transparent user consent handling
