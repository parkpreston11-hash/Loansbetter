
Update the LoansBetter results page (pre-approval / refinance results screen) into a simplified, high-conversion UI.

The goal is to reduce cognitive overload while still maintaining strong financial clarity and trust.

The design should feel like:
- Stripe / Apple-level clean fintech UI
- modern, minimal, and structured
- easy to scan in under 10 seconds
- mobile-first responsive

DO NOT show too many sections at once.
Use progressive disclosure (collapsed sections for advanced details).

--------------------------------------------------
1. MAIN RESULT CARD (PRIMARY FOCUS)
--------------------------------------------------

At the top, show a single clean result card:

Title:
“Refinancing could lower your monthly payment”

Show:
- Estimated monthly savings: $XXX – $XXX (large and prominent)
- Simple explanation (1–2 lines max):

“Your current rate is above today’s market average. Refinancing could reduce your monthly payment and lower total interest over time.”

Keep this section very clean and easy to read.

--------------------------------------------------
2. DECISION CLARITY BADGE
--------------------------------------------------

Add a small indicator under the main result:

“Decision Level: Low Complexity (1/3)”

Subtext:
“This is a relatively straightforward financial decision.”

Use subtle styling (no aggressive colors).

--------------------------------------------------
3. KEY INSIGHT SECTION (MINIMAL)
--------------------------------------------------

Show only 2 bullet points:

- You may save money by refinancing now
- Closing costs are typically recovered within 18–24 months

Keep it short and non-overwhelming.

--------------------------------------------------
4. OPTIONAL EXPANDABLE SECTION (COLLAPSED BY DEFAULT)
--------------------------------------------------

Add a button:
“See detailed comparison”

Inside expand:

Show side-by-side options:

Refinance now:
- Lower monthly payment
- Lock in current rate
- Closing costs apply

Wait for rates:
- No fees now
- Rates may or may not improve

Cash-out refinance:
- Access home equity
- Higher loan balance

Keep this hidden by default.

--------------------------------------------------
5. OPTIONAL TOOL SECTION (COLLAPSED)
--------------------------------------------------

Label:
“What if I change my situation?”

Allow sliders or inputs:
- income change
- debt change
- credit improvement

This should dynamically update estimated range.

Keep this fully hidden unless opened.

--------------------------------------------------
6. BOTTOM CTA SECTION (CRITICAL)
--------------------------------------------------

At the bottom of the page ONLY include two primary actions:

SECTION 1:
Title: “Talk to a Loan Officer”
Description: “Get a personalized breakdown from a licensed professional.”
Button: “Talk to a Loan Officer”

SECTION 2:
Title: “Chat with AI Assistant”
Description: “Ask questions and explore your options instantly.”
Button: “Chat with AI Assistant”

These must be the ONLY main actions on the page.

--------------------------------------------------
7. UX RULES
--------------------------------------------------

- One main idea per screen
- Reduce visual clutter
- Use progressive disclosure for advanced info
- Keep typography clean and readable
- Use soft shadows, rounded cards, and subtle animations
- Avoid overwhelming users with too many numbers at once

--------------------------------------------------
FINAL GOAL
--------------------------------------------------

The user should be able to understand their result in under 10 seconds and feel:

“This is simple, clear, and I know what to do next.”

The interface should increase confidence and guide action without pressure.
