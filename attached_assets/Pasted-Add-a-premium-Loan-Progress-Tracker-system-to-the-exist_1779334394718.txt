Add a premium “Loan Progress Tracker” system to the existing LoansBetter website inspired by the clarity and simplicity of USPS package tracking, Uber ride progress, and modern fintech dashboards.

IMPORTANT:
This is NOT meant to feel like a boring banking portal or outdated mortgage dashboard.

The experience should feel:
- modern
- reassuring
- transparent
- easy to understand
- calm and professional

The goal is to reduce user anxiety during the mortgage process by clearly showing:
- where they are
- what is happening
- what comes next
- estimated timelines

DO NOT redesign the whole site.
Integrate this feature naturally into the current LoansBetter design system.

OVERALL DESIGN STYLE:
- Stripe / Apple / Rocket Mortgage inspired
- Clean white and soft gray backgrounds
- Rounded cards
- Soft shadows
- Smooth animations
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Subtle blue/green accent colors
- Premium fintech aesthetic
- Minimal but not flat or boring

MAIN PAGE TITLE:
“Track your mortgage progress with clarity.”

SUBTEXT:
“Understand where you are in the process, what’s happening now, and what to expect next.”

--------------------------------------------------
CORE FEATURE: INTERACTIVE LOAN TIMELINE
--------------------------------------------------

Create a modern horizontal or vertical progress tracker showing all major loan stages.

STAGES:
1. Application Submitted
2. Documents Received
3. Underwriting Review
4. Conditional Approval
5. Final Approval
6. Escrow / Closing
7. Funding Complete

IMPORTANT:
Each stage must:
- visually light up when completed
- show current active stage clearly
- show locked/future stages subtly faded
- include icons
- have smooth transitions/animations

CURRENT STAGE CARD:
When user clicks or taps a stage, open a clean detailed card showing:

1. Stage Name
2. Simple explanation of what is happening
3. Typical estimated timeline
4. “What you can do now” section
5. Reassurance message

EXAMPLE:

Stage:
Underwriting Review

What’s happening:
“Your financial information and documents are currently being reviewed to verify eligibility and finalize loan details.”

Typical timeline:
“Usually completed within 2–5 business days.”

What you can do now:
“Keep requested documents accessible in case additional information is needed.”

Reassurance:
“This stage is a normal part of the mortgage process.”

--------------------------------------------------
ESTIMATED TIMELINE SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------

Add a visual estimated completion section.

Example:
Estimated closing window:
“May 28 – June 2”

OR:
Estimated time remaining:
“3–5 business days”

IMPORTANT:
Include disclaimer text:
“Timelines are estimates and may vary depending on documentation and lender processing.”

Do NOT guarantee dates.

--------------------------------------------------
“WHAT HAPPENS NEXT” SECTION
--------------------------------------------------

Under every stage, include:

“What happens next”

Example:
“Once underwriting review is completed, your application may move into conditional approval.”

Purpose:
Reduce uncertainty and increase user confidence.

--------------------------------------------------
PROGRESS CONFIDENCE INDICATOR
--------------------------------------------------

Add a small confidence/progress clarity indicator.

Example:
- Early Stage
- Mid Process
- Near Completion

Include a short explanation:
“You are progressing normally through the mortgage process.”

IMPORTANT:
Avoid red warning/error styles unless necessary.

The experience should feel reassuring, not stressful.

--------------------------------------------------
MICRO INTERACTIONS
--------------------------------------------------

Add subtle premium interactions:
- timeline nodes animate softly
- hover effects on stages
- smooth progress fill animation
- fade-in details panel
- soft card elevation on interaction

DO NOT:
- use flashy motion
- use aggressive alerts
- create a gaming-style interface

Goal:
Premium fintech experience.

--------------------------------------------------
OPTIONAL FEATURE: SAVE & RETURN
--------------------------------------------------

Add a small section:
“Save your progress and revisit anytime.”

Optional button:
“Email my progress summary”

Purpose:
Increase return visits and user retention.

--------------------------------------------------
MOBILE EXPERIENCE (VERY IMPORTANT)
--------------------------------------------------

The tracker must work perfectly on phones.

Mobile version should:
- stack cleanly
- remain readable
- keep timeline intuitive
- avoid clutter

--------------------------------------------------
FINAL USER EXPERIENCE GOAL
--------------------------------------------------

When users interact with this tracker, they should feel:

“I understand exactly where I am, what’s happening, and what comes next.”

The experience should reduce stress and build trust through clarity and transparency.

The system should feel like:
- a modern fintech product

Focus on:
- clarity
