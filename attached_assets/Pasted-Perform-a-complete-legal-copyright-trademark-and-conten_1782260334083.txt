Perform a complete legal, copyright, trademark, and content compliance audit of this entire website.

DO NOT change the design, layout, styling, branding, functionality, SEO structure, animations, forms, database connections, or user experience unless required to resolve a compliance issue.

Audit every page, component, image, icon, logo, testimonial, statistic, claim, and text element.

CHECK FOR:

1. COPYRIGHT ISSUES

* Images copied from Google Images.
* Images from competitor websites.
* Watermarked images.
* Unlicensed stock photos.
* Copyrighted graphics or illustrations.
* Copyrighted text copied from other websites.

2. TRADEMARK ISSUES

* Logos belonging to companies that do not own or authorize their use.
* Third-party trademarks used without permission.
* Brand names, graphics, or visual assets that may create infringement risk.

3. FALSE OR UNVERIFIED CLAIMS

* Customer counts.
* Websites sold.
* Revenue claims.
* Ratings.
* Awards.
* Rankings.
* Testimonials.
* Success rates.
* Guarantees.
* Performance statistics.
* Any statement that cannot be reasonably verified.

4. TESTIMONIAL COMPLIANCE

* Identify testimonials that appear fictional, unverified, or misleading.
* Flag any reviews that may require client approval.
* Do not invent new testimonials.

5. REAL ESTATE COMPLIANCE

* Property photos that may not have usage rights.
* Listing information that may be inaccurate.
* Misleading real estate claims.

6. BUSINESS COMPLIANCE

* Missing contact information.
* Missing privacy policy if forms collect personal information.
* Misleading marketing language.
* Unsupported guarantees or promises.

REQUIRED ACTIONS:

A. Generate a detailed report listing:

* Every potential issue found.
* Exact page location.
* Exact text or image involved.
* Reason it may create legal, copyright, trademark, licensing, or advertising risk.

B. For IMAGES ONLY:

* If an image appears to be unsafe, copyrighted, scraped, or unlicensed:
    * Replace it automatically with a commercially usable alternative from approved sources.
    * Use only royalty-free, commercially licensed, or AI-generated images suitable for commercial use.
    * Preserve the same design quality and visual intent.

C. For TEXT:

* DO NOT automatically rewrite content.
* Instead, list each issue in the audit report and explain why it may be problematic.
* Provide a recommended replacement but wait for approval before changing text.

D. Create a final summary showing:

* Number of image issues found.
* Number of text issues found.
* Number of compliance risks found.
* Overall website risk level (Low, Moderate, High).

The goal is to make the website as safe as reasonably possible for commercial use while preserving the existing design and functionality.
